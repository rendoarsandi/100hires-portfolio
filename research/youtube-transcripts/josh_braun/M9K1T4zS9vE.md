## Core Outreach Takeaways & Summary

*   **Personalization**: Never use generic templates. Always research prospect LinkedIn profiles or recent news hooks.
*   **Friction-Free Call to Action**: Ask for a simple reply or feedback (e.g. 'Open to checking it out?') instead of demanding a 30-minute meeting.
*   **Deliverability Rules**: Spin up secondary domains (never use main domains for outreach), set up SPF, DKIM, DMARC, and custom tracking domains.
*   **Message Length**: Keep emails under 75-100 words. Make them easy to read on mobile devices.
