# Radically Honest Cold Calls

**Source**: [LinkedIn Post](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/joshbraun_coldcalling-sales-tips)
**Date**: 2026-06-22

## Post Content

When someone answers a cold call, they are immediately on the defensive. They know it's a sales pitch.

Defuse the tension by being radically honest.

'Hi Sarah, this is Josh. We've never spoken before, and to be completely transparent, this is a cold call. I know you're busy. Would you like to hang up now, or give me 30 seconds to explain why I called, and then decide?'

9 out of 10 times, they laugh and say 'Go ahead.'

Permission-based selling works.
