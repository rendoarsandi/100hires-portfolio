# Poke the Bear Framework

**Source**: [LinkedIn Post](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/joshbraun_sales-prospecting-coldemail)
**Date**: 2026-06-18

## Post Content

Traditional pitching: 'Hi, we are the leading provider of X and we help companies do Y...'

Result: Delete.

Instead of pitching, 'poke the bear'. State a common, painful problem in a neutral tone and ask if they are experiencing it.

Example:
'Most VP of Sales I talk to tell me their reps are spending 3 hours a day manually researching leads instead of calling them.

How are you making sure your reps stay focused on selling?'

This starts a dialogue, not a sales pitch.
