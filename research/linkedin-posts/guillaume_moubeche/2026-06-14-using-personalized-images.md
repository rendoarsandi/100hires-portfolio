# Using Personalized Images

**Source**: [LinkedIn Post](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/guillaumemoubeche_lemlist-saas-growth)
**Date**: 2026-06-14

## Post Content

People buy from people, not logos.

Instead of sending plain text, we use lemlist's dynamic image generation to show a photo of the sales rep holding a coffee cup with the prospect's company logo on it.

Reply rates went from 8% to 22%.

Personalization isn't just about putting their name in the subject line. It's about showing that you actually took 5 minutes to think about them.
