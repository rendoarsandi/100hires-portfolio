# Cold Email Scale Playbook

**Source**: [LinkedIn Post](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/nickabraham_smartlead-outbound-leadgen)
**Date**: 2026-06-10

## Post Content

How we send 50,000 cold emails a month for B2B SaaS clients:

- 50 secondary domains.
- 100 total inboxes (2 per domain).
- Smartlead for master inbox rotation and sequence orchestration.
- Clay for list enrichment and custom AI-written opening lines.
- Double verification: Run lists through NeverBounce + Scrubby (to catch catch-all emails).

If you don't build this level of infrastructure, you'll land in spam by week 3.
