# Cold Email Teardown: Before and After

**Source**: [LinkedIn Post](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/floriandecludt_copywriting-coldemail-teardown)
**Date**: 2026-06-17

## Post Content

BEFORE:
'Dear Sir/Madam, we are a leading software agency offering state of the art design and development. We would love to hop on a call to see how we can assist you...'

AFTER:
'Hi John, noticed your company just hired 3 new engineers. Are you still spending hours onboarding them onto your legacy codebase, or did you automate that workflow?'

Keep it focused on their situation, not your offer.
