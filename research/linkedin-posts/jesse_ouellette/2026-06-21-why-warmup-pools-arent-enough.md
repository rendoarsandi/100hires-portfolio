# Why Warmup Pools Aren't Enough

**Source**: [LinkedIn Post](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/jesseouellette_deliverability-spf-dkim)
**Date**: 2026-06-21

## Post Content

Many people think they can buy a domain, throw it in a warmup pool for 2 weeks, and send 500 emails a day.

That era is over. ESPs (Google & Microsoft) are tracking sender reputation based on domain age, traffic patterns, and spam reports.

Warmup pools simulate positive engagement, but if your actual campaigns have a high bounce rate or low reply rate, your domain reputation will plummet instantly.

Focus on data quality. A clean list is the best deliverability tool.
