# The 3-Step Follow-Up Sequence

**Source**: [LinkedIn Post](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/jedmahrle_prospecting-outbound-sales)
**Date**: 2026-06-12

## Post Content

Stop sending 'just bumping this' follow-ups. They add zero value.

Here is a 3-step follow-up sequence that gets replies:

Day 1: The Core Pitch (Focus on problem + trigger).
Day 3: The Asset Drop ('Saw you publish X, thought this guide on Y might be useful. No pitch.').
Day 7: The Direct Question ('Are you still focusing on X this quarter, or should I stop reaching out?').

Every touchpoint should either teach them something new or make it extremely easy to say 'No'.
