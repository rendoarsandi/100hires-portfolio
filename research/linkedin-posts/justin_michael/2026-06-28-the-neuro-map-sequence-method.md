# The Neuro-Map Sequence Method

**Source**: [LinkedIn Post](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/justinmichael_outbound-sales-jmm)
**Date**: 2026-06-28

## Post Content

To break through to enterprise C-suite buyers, you need a high-frequency, multi-channel approach.

My Neuro-Map JMM method:
- Day 1: LinkedIn connection request + view profile.
- Day 1 (10 mins later): Cold email (short, mobile-optimized).
- Day 2: Cold call + VM (no pitch, just direct problem reference).
- Day 4: LinkedIn message.
- Day 7: Direct email with a quick video walk-through.

Be persistent. It takes 12-15 touches to get through to a busy executive.
