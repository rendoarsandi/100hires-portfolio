# The No-Friction CTA

**Source**: [LinkedIn Post](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/willallred_spam-deliverability-sales)
**Date**: 2026-06-20

## Post Content

Most cold email CTAs fail because they ask for too much, too soon.

'Are you free for 30 minutes on Thursday at 2 PM?'

This is a huge ask for a stranger. You are asking them to commit time they don't have.

Instead, use a low-friction CTA:
- 'Open to checking it out?'
- 'Would you want to see the template we used?'
- 'Is this on your radar for Q3?'

Lower the bar. Get the reply first. You can schedule the meeting later.
