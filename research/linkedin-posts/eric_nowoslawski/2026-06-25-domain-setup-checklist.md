# Domain Setup Checklist

**Source**: [LinkedIn Post](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/ericnowoslawski_deliverability-domain-coldemail)
**Date**: 2026-06-25

## Post Content

If you are sending cold emails from your primary company domain, you are playing Russian roulette with your brand.

Always buy secondary domains. Here's my checklist:
1. Buy domains on Porkbun or Cloudflare (e.g. if company is 'getxyz.com', buy 'tryxyz.com' or 'usexyz.com').
2. Set up Google Workspace or Microsoft 365 (max 2-3 inboxes per domain).
3. Configure DNS records: SPF, DKIM, and DMARC (p=quarantine or p=none to start).
4. Warm up inboxes for 14-21 days using smart tools like Smartlead.
5. Keep daily sending limit to 30-40 emails per inbox maximum.
